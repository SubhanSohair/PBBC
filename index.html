About PBBC

The Pakistan Britain Business Council (PBBC) has a broad mandate to foster and develop bilateral trade between Pakistan and the United Kingdom. As an intermediary, PBBC facilitates connections among businesses, entrepreneurs, professional advisers, and government agencies in Britain, Pakistan, and other jurisdictions to promote mutual trade opportunities.

PBBC is committed to hosting trade events and networking opportunities across the UK and Pakistan. It also aims to organise regular trade missions to Pakistan with a specific industry focus, highlighting Pakistan as a secure and attractive destination for business.

Become A Member

For larger businesses being Corporate Members enjoy privileged access to high profile events, meetings with senior government ministers (both UK and Pakistan), participation in high level official trade missions, enhanced support and also networking receptions. Additional benefits: Access to PBBC platform with an invitation to speak at events and forums. Opportunities for sponsoring PBBC events, exhibitions and participation in official visits.

Apply Now

PBBC facilitates members to develop explore, grow and enhance their trade and businesses both in Britain and Pakistan. Members benefit from B2B meetings, forums, business networking events, with a prospect of interacting with visiting business and official delegates. Furthermore PBBC introduces and provides opportunities to businesses to exhibit or showcase their goods and services.

Apply Now

PBBC is committed to encouraging and supporting small and medium-sized enterprises as they are a significant contributor to the wider economy. Our SME programme offers tailored support and advice for small and medium size businesses with our forums and events. PBBC is a focused advisory platform enabling SME companies to consider trade and investment in Pakistan and to share practical advice and support.
